Information We Collect

1. Personal Information You Provide

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide when using our App:

Customer Information

  • Customer Details: Names, phone numbers, email addresses, physical addresses
  • Business Contact Information: Company names, business addresses, contact persons
  • Financial Information: Account balances, credit/debit amounts, outstanding balances

User Account Information

  • Authentication Data: Email addresses, passwords (securely encrypted)
  • Profile Information: User preferences, settings, business configurations

Transaction Data

  • Sales Records: Product names, quantities, prices, dates, times

2. Automatically Collected Information

Device Information

  • Technical Data: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • App Usage Data: App interactions, feature usage patterns, session duration
  • Performance Data: App crashes, error logs, loading times

Network Information

  • Connection Data: IP addresses, network type (WiFi/cellular)
  • Location Data: General geographic location (city/region level) for analytics

3. Business Data

Product Information

  • Inventory Data: Product names, SKUs, categories, pricing, stock quantities
  • Catalog Information: Product descriptions, images, cost data
  • Stock Management: Reorder levels, supplier information

Financial Records

  • Ledger Entries: Transaction histories, account balances, payment records
  • Revenue Data: Sales figures, profit margins, business analytics
  • Reporting Data: Performance metrics, trend analysis, business insights

Physical Security
